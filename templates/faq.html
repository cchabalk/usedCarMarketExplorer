Brief Description

      Prices in the user car market car fluctuate based on many factors including gas prices, the introduction of a new technology, or a recall event like non-compliant emissions. Pricing suggestions can be obtained through several websites, however, detailed overview information and pricing trends are not easily available.

      This website offers a portal through which to view the current asking prices of used cars. This information provides the user with an overview of the current trends of prices for a given mileage and option package. Buyers will find this information useful as a starting point to determine potential vehicles. In addition, the classification algorithm allows buyers to determine weather a particular vehicle price is excessively above or below the typical range, which may prompt further investigation.

      Sellers will find the information useful when choosing a price point relative to what is available elsewhere. For a particular car, the seller can choose a price at the lower end of the range for a quick sale, or a higher price knowing that the automobile may justify added salesmanship.

      Vehicles are classified in terms of pricing trends, rather than a static number like price. The trend characterization is derived from market forces, and is a natural and well suited metric for classification.
